Facebook Data Deletion Instructions

How to remove your data

Suaralanre solutions uses your whatsapp business account to send messages to your customers.

SuaraLanre solutions does not save your personal data in our server.

According to Facebook policy, we have to provide User Data Deletion Callback URL or Data Deletion Instructions URL. ‍

If you want to delete your activities with us, you can remove your information by following these steps:

  • Go to your Facebook Account’s “Settings & Privacy”.
  • Click “Settings”
  • Then click “Security and Login”.
  • Look for “Apps and Websites” and you will see all of the apps and websites you have linked with your Facebook account.
  • Search and Click Suaralanre solutions in the search bar.
  • Scroll and click “Remove”.
